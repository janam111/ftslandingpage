Failure to success is an initiative taken to start treating success and failure hand in hand.

FTS in association with “Janta Seva Mandal, Ballia Uttar Pradesh”, has been started in 2020 to serve people by normalising failures and uplifting an individual’s growth.
We are here to make people share not just their success stories,but also their failures.

What we do

The more we talk about and hear stories of failure, the more at ease we will be about putting our toe in the water, raising our hand, trying that new thing and taking the leap. Stop keeping failures hidden. Let’s bring them out into the light and see them for what they really are opportunities for learning and growing.
We offer Internships, Motivation and Seminard for the stuents to enhance their skills overcome their failures.

We are an Organisation

FTS is basically made to make people aware about how important it is to share our failures and also accept them.

  • At a time, where everybody is busy sharing only their success, it makes a person question their capability who couldn't succeed and they slowly get depressed.
  • FTS is a platform to make people realize that success and failure go together and one should treat both in a normal way.
  • Success comes with failures and it's an indispensable part of life as Failures are just delay, not defeat.
  • FTS empowers everyone to face their failures with the same spirit they celebrate their success with
